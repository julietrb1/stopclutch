Frequently asked questions

What is Stopclutch for?

Stopclutch is a website that holds the fastest race times from players of racing games{% if first_game %} such as {% modelimage first_game.image 'makelogo-list' first_game.name %} {{ first_game.name }}{% endif %} , and allows for comparison and showcasing of said race times by several means. It was born from the desire to keep track of who was the fastest in a group of friends, and quickly grew into a website published online.

Who made it?

I did! My name's Dylan, and I'm a car/motorbike enthusiast from New Zealand. I'm currently embarking on a Bachelor of Engineering (Software), hence the whole website thing and all. I enjoy projects that have a positive practical impact on people (i.e. making things people want to use), and I'm hoping this is one such project.

Who gets to be a player?

Anyone fortunate enough to have had a go on my racing wheel and to set a time that got uploaded for testing purposes. There's no better data than real data, so they were kind enough to contribute their times.

Can I use Stopclutch?

Sure, come visit me. But seriously, it's currently a "closed" project in the sense that it's not technically open for complete public consumption. Obviously you're consuming it if you're reading this, but it's read-only data for the timebeing. After gauging public interest regarding others submitting their times, I'll consider making it available for the public.
